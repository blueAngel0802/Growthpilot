# SEO/SEM Strategy

## SEO foundations

- Clear H1/H2/H3 structure
- Metadata helper for title, description, canonical, Open Graph, and Twitter metadata
- Sitemap and robots files
- CMS-style blog content model
- Internal linking opportunities between blog, services, audit, and campaign pages
- FAQ and educational content plan
- Performance-minded layout without external font dependency

## SEM landing page approach

- One primary conversion goal per landing page
- Message match between ad copy and hero section
- CTA above the fold and repeated after proof/FAQ sections
- Short lead form to reduce friction
- Campaign-specific sections for trust, benefits, objections, and FAQs
- GTM/GA4 event tracking for CTA clicks, form starts, form submissions, phone clicks, pricing views, and qualified leads

## Conversion optimization

- Hero outcome statement
- Clear CTA hierarchy
- Trust and proof blocks
- Short forms
- Funnel metrics
- Lead scoring and priority assignment
- Client-facing recommendations based on channel performance
